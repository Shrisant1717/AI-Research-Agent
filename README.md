# 🤖 AI Research Agent

An AI-powered multi-agent research automation system that streamlines the entire research workflow—from paper discovery and literature review to research gap identification and report generation.

## 🚀 Overview

AI Research Agent is a multi-agent system built using Large Language Models (LLMs), LangGraph, and LangChain to automate academic and technical research tasks. The platform retrieves relevant research papers from scholarly sources, analyzes their content, generates concise summaries, identifies research gaps, and produces structured research reports.

The goal is to reduce the time spent on manual literature reviews and help researchers, students, and professionals accelerate their research process.

## 🔄 Workflow

### Step 1: User Research Query

The user provides a research topic or problem statement.

### Step 2: Paper Retrieval

Relevant research papers are fetched using the arXiv API.

### Step 3: Paper Analysis

The system extracts content from PDFs and processes the text.

### Step 4: Summarization

Key findings and contributions are summarized.

### Step 5: Research Gap Detection

The system identifies limitations and unexplored areas.

### Step 6: Insight Generation

Novel ideas and research directions are proposed.

### Step 7: Report Generation

A structured research report is generated automatically.
